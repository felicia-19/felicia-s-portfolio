CliniCompass — Rare Disease Next-Step Copilot

Team Project · HackRare @ Harvard · 2026 - 2026

Built an in-house platform to help clinicians decide what to do next in rare genetic disease diagnoses. The clinician enters HPO terms plus key context (onset/severity, family history, prior testing), and the system surfaces similar cases and produces a ranked list of feasible next steps (tests, referrals, additional phenotyping). The core retrieval logic supports both pre- and post-sequencing workflows (phenotype→gene ranking; phenotype+variant→disease ranking; and negative-lead case matching).
